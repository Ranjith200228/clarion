# New patient intake

## What new patients must provide before booking

New patients must provide:

- Full legal name and date of birth
- Insurance carrier and member id (we verify eligibility before the visit)
- A callback phone number
- Reason for visit (chief complaint)

If the patient cannot provide insurance information on the call, book the
appointment but flag the chart for the front desk to follow up before the
visit date.

## Which appointment types accept new patients

The following appointment types are open to new patients:

- Cataract Pre-Op Consult
- Diabetic Retinopathy Screening
- Routine Eye Exam

Glaucoma Follow-Up and Post-Op LASIK Follow-Up are established patients only.

## Provider assignment for new patients

Dr. Smith and Dr. Chen accept new patients. Dr. Patel is not accepting new
patients at this time. If a new patient asks specifically for Dr. Patel,
offer Dr. Smith for cataract or Dr. Chen for routine, and create a task for
the office manager.
