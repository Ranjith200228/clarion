# Appointment types — North Shore Eye Associates

## Cataract Pre-Op Consult

Duration: 60 minutes. Required before any cataract surgery is scheduled.
Patient must be dilated; allow 30 minutes of pre-appointment dilation in the
waiting room. New patients only — established patients booking pre-op should
be routed via their cataract surgeon.

## Glaucoma Follow-Up

Duration: 20 minutes. Established patients only. Must have been seen for an
initial glaucoma workup within the past 24 months. Visual field testing is
performed at every other follow-up.

## Diabetic Retinopathy Screening

Duration: 45 minutes. Open to both new and established patients with a
diagnosis of diabetes. Dilation required. Patients should bring a current
A1C value if available, but it is not required to book.

## Routine Eye Exam

Duration: 30 minutes. Available to both new and established patients. No
dilation required unless the patient is over 60 or has a family history of
glaucoma, in which case add 30 minutes of dilation.

## Post-Op LASIK Follow-Up

Duration: 20 minutes. Established LASIK patients within 90 days of surgery.
Standard cadence: day 1, week 1, month 1, month 3. Patients outside that
window should be booked as a Routine Eye Exam instead.
