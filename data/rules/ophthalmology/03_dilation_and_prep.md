# Pre-appointment prep

## Dilation requirements

Dilation is required for the following appointment types:

- Cataract Pre-Op Consult — always
- Diabetic Retinopathy Screening — always
- Routine Eye Exam — only if patient is 60 or older, or has a family history of glaucoma

When dilation is required, the patient must:

- Arrive 30 minutes early for drops
- Bring sunglasses for the trip home
- Arrange transportation home (vision will be blurry for 4-6 hours)
- Not plan to drive themselves after the visit

The agent should explicitly ask the patient about transportation when
booking any dilated visit, and create a task if no transport is confirmed.

## What patients should bring

- Photo ID
- Insurance card
- A list of all current medications (including drops)
- Their current glasses or contact lens prescription, if available
