# Cancellation and reschedule policy

## Cancellation window

We ask for 24 hours' notice for cancellations. The agent may cancel an
appointment on the patient's behalf for any reason — no human intervention
is needed unless the patient is canceling within 24 hours of a procedure
(see below).

## Same-day or short-notice cancellations

If the patient is canceling within 24 hours of:

- Cataract Pre-Op Consult — create a task for the surgical scheduling team
- Any other appointment type — proceed and offer to reschedule

## Reschedule policy

Rescheduling is allowed without restriction up to 24 hours before the
appointment. After that, the agent should cancel and offer to book a new
appointment.

## No-show policy

We do not penalize a first no-show. After two no-shows, the patient must
speak with the office manager before booking again — the agent should not
attempt to book in that case and instead create a task.
