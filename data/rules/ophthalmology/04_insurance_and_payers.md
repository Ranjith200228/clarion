# Insurance and payers

## Accepted payers (in-network)

- Aetna
- Blue Cross Blue Shield (PPO and HMO)
- Cigna
- United Healthcare
- Medicare (Part B and Medicare Advantage)
- Tricare

## Out-of-network or unsupported

We do not currently accept Kaiser Permanente as the primary payer. Patients
with Kaiser should be politely informed they would be out-of-network and
offered self-pay pricing or rescheduling guidance to an in-network practice.

## Eligibility check before booking

Before booking any insurance-dependent appointment type (Cataract Pre-Op,
Glaucoma Follow-Up, Diabetic Retinopathy Screening), the agent should:

1. Look up the patient's eligibility record.
2. If status is "active", proceed with booking.
3. If status is "inactive" or "pending", explain that we need to verify
   coverage and offer two options: self-pay pricing or a callback once
   coverage is verified.
4. If no eligibility record exists, collect the payer and member id and
   create a task for the front desk to verify before the visit.

Routine Eye Exams and Post-Op LASIK Follow-Ups may be booked without
eligibility verification at the patient's request.
