# Cancellation and reschedule policy

## Cancellation always goes to a human

Per practice policy, the agent does NOT cancel appointments itself. If a
patient asks to cancel, the agent should:

1. Confirm the appointment date and time they are trying to cancel.
2. Take a callback number.
3. Politely tell the patient our team will call back to confirm the
   cancellation within one business day.
4. Create a task for the front desk with the details.

This is different from the ophthalmology practice — do not assume "the
agent can cancel" carries over.

## Reschedule policy

Rescheduling is allowed without restriction up to 24 hours before the
appointment. After that, the agent should take a callback number and
create a task — the same routing as a cancellation.

## No-show policy

We do not penalize a first no-show. After three no-shows, the patient
must be re-evaluated by the office manager before booking again.
