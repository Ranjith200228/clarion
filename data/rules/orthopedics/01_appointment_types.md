# Appointment types — Summit Orthopedic Partners

## New Patient Joint Consult

Duration: 45 minutes. For patients presenting with joint pain or limited
mobility who have not been seen by us before. Recent imaging (within 90
days) should be uploaded to the patient portal before the visit if
available.

## Post-Op Follow-Up

Duration: 20 minutes. Established surgical patients only. Standard
post-operative cadence is week 2, week 6, and month 3 unless the surgeon
has specified otherwise.

## Sports Medicine Consult

Duration: 30 minutes. New or established patients with sport-related injury
or performance concern. We accept athletic-trainer referrals from local
high schools and colleges.

## Physical Therapy Referral

Duration: 30 minutes. This is the in-house intake visit with a PT to scope
a program. Patient must have an existing provider referral or an active
diagnosis on file.

## Imaging Review (X-ray / MRI)

Duration: 30 minutes. Patient is reviewing prior or recently completed
imaging with an orthopedist. Imaging must be on file in the portal at least
24 hours before the visit so the provider has time to review.
