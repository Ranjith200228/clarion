# New patient intake

## What new patients must provide before booking

New patients must provide:

- Full legal name and date of birth
- Insurance carrier and member id (or Workers' Compensation claim number)
- A callback phone number
- A short description of the affected joint and how long the issue has been
  ongoing

For sports medicine consults, also collect:

- Sport and competitive level (recreational, high school, collegiate)
- Whether the patient is in-season

## Which appointment types accept new patients

The following appointment types are open to new patients:

- New Patient Joint Consult
- Sports Medicine Consult
- Imaging Review (X-ray / MRI) — only if the patient is bringing outside imaging

Post-Op Follow-Up and Physical Therapy Referral are established patients
only.

## Provider assignment

Dr. Garcia and Dr. Singh accept new patients. Dr. O'Connor is post-op only
and should not be booked for new patient visits — route new patients to
Dr. Garcia for general orthopedics or Dr. Singh for sports.
