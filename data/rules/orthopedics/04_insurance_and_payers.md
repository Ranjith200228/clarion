# Insurance and payers

## Accepted payers (in-network)

- Aetna
- Cigna
- United Healthcare (Choice and Choice Plus)
- Anthem Blue Cross
- Workers' Compensation (any carrier — see workers comp rules)

## Out-of-network or unsupported

We do not currently accept Medicaid in this practice. Patients with
Medicaid should be politely informed they would be out-of-network and
offered self-pay pricing or referral guidance to a community clinic that
accepts their plan.

## Eligibility check before booking

For all non-workers-comp patients, look up the eligibility record before
booking any appointment type other than a Sports Medicine Consult (which we
will see on self-pay if needed). If the eligibility status is "inactive",
explain the situation and offer self-pay pricing or rescheduling once
coverage is verified.
